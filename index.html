Fintual challenge 🏅

Construct a simple Portfolio class that has a collection of Stocks and a “Profit” method that receives 2 dates and retuns the profit of the Portfolio between those dates. Assume each Stock has a “Price” method that receives a date and returns its price.

1. Construct a simple Portfolio class 💼

  1. That has a collection of Stocks
  2. And a “Profit” method

2. The "Profit" method 🤑

  1. It'll be able to receives 2 dates
  2. The method will retuns the profit of the Portfolio between those dates

3. Each Stock has a “Price” method 💰

  1. The method receives a date
  2. The method returns the stock price.

S. 🔥 Bonus Track

  • Make the Profit method return the “annualized return” of the portfolio between the given dates.
